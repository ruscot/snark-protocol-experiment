\\ We define 3 methods Setup, Prove and Verify such that :
\\$Setup(R) \rightarrow (\sigma,\tau)$ 
\\$Prove(R, \sigma, a_{i...l}, a_{l+1...m}) \rightarrow \pi$
\\$Verify(R, \sigma, a_{i...l}, \pi) \rightarrow 0/1$
